18-Month Performance Roadmap

Recover. Rebuild. Scale.

AMA aims to regain lost revenue from 2025 and re-stabilize paid media and SEO performance to create further investment opportunity. The focus then shifts away from tactical and technical inefficiencies — and toward a much better telling of the AMA story and brand promise. This roadmap addresses both the technical/tactical and strategic messaging needs in parallel: generating near-term revenue recovery while building the digital and brand infrastructure needed for durable growth.

Website Assessment

The current site has three compounding problems that limit what can be fixed before a rebuild. Technical SEO fixes within the current CMS will produce real gains and inform the new build, but they have a ceiling.

Today's Constraints

Custom CMS blocks key technical SEO work. Cookiebot alone blocks 58 scripts from Google's crawler; schema and structured data are difficult to implement at scale.

The site is not built to convert a $2,600 purchase: no outcome proof, no structured journey from research to registration, UX that predates modern buyer expectations.

Performance is inconsistent day-to-day with limited diagnostic visibility in the current environment.

What the New Site Solves

Built on a modern stack: technical SEO by default (crawlable, indexed, structured data native).

UX designed around a high-consideration purchase: outcome-led, instructor-credibility-forward, clear path from research to registration.

Brand story can be fully told. The $2,600 price point requires it. CRO testing infrastructure built in from day one.

Comprehensive Measurement Framework

Live and automatically updated Looker dashboards are built to answer how front-end marketing efforts are converting into back-end revenue results. Reports are available at any time and formally reviewed on a bi-weekly basis.

Weekly Tracking

Daily revenue vs. $32K baseline and $50K target. Paid media spend pacing, ROAS by campaign type. Top keyword position movement and non-brand impression share.

Monthly Reporting (Pulse)

Full-funnel attribution: CPA by channel. AI citation share of voice vs. baseline. SFDC pipeline attributed to marketing. Organic traffic and conversion contribution.

Quarterly Reviews

Revenue trend vs. target. Organic share of total conversions. Brand lift indicators (branded search volume, direct traffic). Strategic assessment: what's working, what adjusts next quarter.

Consistent, Proactive Campaign Optimization

Each day, week, month, and quarter our media buyers perform optimizations proactively, check anomalies, and find opportunities to present and take advantage of.

Daily Operations

Platform health checks. Budget & pacing management. Performance monitoring — review KPI shifts and flag optimization opportunities. Data hygiene: reconcile conversion and spend data, maintain tracking integrity.

Weekly & Monthly Optimization

Bid adjustments and search term analysis. Performance trend reviews across Search, Display, and Paid Social. Creative & landing page testing — evaluate experiments and scale winning variants. Budget planning and reporting.

Quarterly Strategic Audits

Comprehensive campaign and platform audits. Competitive benchmarking and market analysis. Strategic recommendations for budget allocation and growth.
